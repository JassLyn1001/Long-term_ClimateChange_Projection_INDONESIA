# Long-term_ClimateChange_Projection_INDONESIA
In this project, the climate modelling team has responsibility to provide high-resolution climate information to help the government  preparing the fourth national communication report to the UNFCCC. We use statistical approach to downscale model output from CMIP6 simulation under 4 future scenarios for atmospheric and ocean climate variables.
